2015–2020

Things to Know

To help you feel at ease and enjoy every moment of the celebrations, we've gathered a few thoughtful details we'd love for you to know before the big day.

🚗
Getting Here

For Kerala events, the nearest airport is Trivandrum International Airport (TRV), approximately 25–30 km from the venue. Taxis and app-based cab services are easily available. Trivandrum Central Railway Station also connects major cities across India. For Mathura celebrations, Mathura Junction Railway Station is about 5 km from Hotel Crystal Inn. The nearest airport is Agra Airport (AGR) or Delhi International Airport (DEL), followed by a comfortable road journey.

🏨
Accommodation

For Kerala events, we recommend hotels near Chinnu's Convention Center. Hotel Chinnu's and Hotel Nakshatra has rooms available for wedding guests. For Mathura, Heera Crystal Inn has rooms available for wedding guests.

👘
Dress Code

Traditional and festive attire is encouraged for all events. Light ethnic wear is ideal for daytime ceremonies, while elegant traditional or formal attire is perfect for evening celebrations. May in Kerala is typically warm and humid (around 28–32°C), with occasional summer showers, so we recommend comfortable fabrics and carrying light rain protection if needed.

🍛
Food & Festivities

A traditional Kerala Sadhya, served on banana leaves, will be part of our Kerala wedding celebrations, celebrating local flavors and customs. At the Mathura reception, guests can look forward to a rich spread of North Indian delicacies along with festive desserts. Come hungry and ready to celebrate!

📸
Photography

Our professional photography team will be capturing every special moment throughout the celebrations. Guests are encouraged to enjoy the festivities while memories are documented for everyone. A shared Google Drive link with photos and videos will be provided after the wedding. Feel free to tag your moments using #ShuBha!

📞
Contact Us

If you need help with travel plans, accommodation, or event details, please reach out to us via WhatsApp. We are happy to assist and ensure your journey and stay are smooth and enjoyable.
